All about Marisa Hayase!

    Did you learn any new hobbies while in quarantine?

    Using the power of YouTube she taught herself how to crochet during lockdown. She ended up crocheting her own stockings in time for the holiday season.

    Who is your favorite Animal Crossings character?

    Marina, the strawberry pink octopus with light yellow cheeks, is Marisa's favorite Animal Crossing character. Her name is from the word marine, a general term for things relating to the sea or ocean.

    The top 3 reasons for enjoying Marina:

    1. She is a great villager
    2. Marina has an energetic personality
    3. She likes to read under apple tree's

    What is life like on the island with Marina?

    Marina has a vibrant personality, and frequently acts kind towards the villagers. She is usally up early in the morning and likes to engage with her fellow friends. She has a lovely taste in furniture which showcases her playful side.

    What's the grossest food you've ever eaten?

    The grossest food Marisa has ever eaten was a Halloween edition flavored ice cream that had bugs in it. Reminded me of the Salt & Straw spooky seasonal ice cream flavor Creepy Crawly Critters, littered with chocolate covered crickets and toffee-brittle mealworms. Mmm, appetizing.
